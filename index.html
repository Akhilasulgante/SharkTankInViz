Visual Landscape of Shark Tank India

Welcome to our website! Here we provide visualizations of data from the popular reality show, Shark Tank India where contestants pitch their ideas to investors, who are addressed as "Sharks," in the hopes of securing funding for their startups. Our objective is to present the data from Shark Tank India in a clear and concise way, allowing users to derive insights and draw their own conclusions. Through our visualizations, we aim to provide a better understanding of the trends and patterns in the show, helping entrepreneurs, investors, and fans alike to stay up-to-date with the latest happenings in the startup world.

Pitches

We analyse the participation of entrepreneurs from various regions of India and the emerging industries in different states as shown on the show. We also explore the differences in valuations offered by Sharks for companies across various industries. Through our analysis of these factors, we aim to gain a better understanding of the range of companies that feature on Shark Tank India.

Investments

We dive into the depths of Shark Tank India, examining the investments made on the show. We conduct a analysis of each shark's individual and joint investments to uncover potential patterns in their decision-making. By creating visualizations, we aim to provide valuable insights for future contestants, enabling them to tailor their pitches to better align with the sharks' investment preferences.
